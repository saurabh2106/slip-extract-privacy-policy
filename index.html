Privacy Policy

Slip Extract
Last updated: April 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Slip Extract ("we", "our", or "the app") is a payslip data extraction tool developed for Android. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and an optional display name. Passwords are managed securely by our authentication provider and are never visible to us.

Usage Data

We store the number of payslip files you have processed and your subscription plan details (including plan name, expiry date, and file limits) to enforce subscription quotas and display your usage accurately within the app.

Crash and Performance Data

We collect anonymous crash reports and performance diagnostics to identify bugs, improve app stability, and enhance user experience. This information does not personally identify users.

Analytics

We collect anonymous and aggregated usage analytics events such as extraction activity, app interactions, and feature usage patterns. No personally identifiable information is attached to these analytics events.

3. Payslip Files and Extracted Data

Uploaded PDF files are securely transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections to our AI processing infrastructure solely for extracting payslip information requested by the user.

Files are processed temporarily and are automatically deleted after processing is completed. No human reviews or accesses user payslip files.

Uploaded files may be processed using trusted third-party AI or OCR infrastructure providers strictly for document text extraction functionality.

We do not permanently store uploaded PDF files on our servers. Extracted payslip data exists only on your device during your session and may be exported by you to Excel.

We do not retain, sell, rent, trade, or transmit your payslip contents to unrelated third parties.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate users and maintain user accounts
  • To enforce subscription plan file usage limits
  • To display account usage and subscription status within the app
  • To process uploaded payslip files requested by users
  • To send password reset emails if requested
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app performance
  • To improve app functionality and user experience

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade user personal information or uploaded documents to third parties.

We share data only with trusted third-party infrastructure providers necessary to operate the app and provide its core functionality. These providers are bound by appropriate security and confidentiality obligations.

6. Third-Party Services

The app may use trusted third-party services including:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

These services may process limited technical information required for authentication, analytics, crash reporting, app stability, and service functionality.

7. Data Retention

Your account-related information, including email address, subscription details, and usage statistics, is retained only as long as your account remains active or as necessary to provide app functionality.

Users may request permanent deletion of their account and associated data at any time by contacting us at slipextract@gmail.com.

8. Permissions Used

Slip Extract may request storage access or document picker permissions solely to allow users to select payslip PDF files for processing.

These permissions are never used to access unrelated personal files, media, or sensitive device data.

9. Security

We use industry-standard cloud security infrastructure and access controls to protect user information.

All communications between the app and backend services are encrypted using secure HTTPS connections.

10. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us. We will respond to reasonable requests within 30 days where applicable.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes may be communicated through an in-app notice or app update. Continued use of the app after policy updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
