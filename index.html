Privacy Policy Slip Extract Last updated: April 21, 2026 1. Introduction Slip Extract ("we", "our", or "the app") is a payslip data extraction tool developed for Android. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. 2. Information We Collect Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and an optional display name. Passwords are managed by our secure authentication provider and are never visible to us. Usage data: We store the number of payslip files you have processed and your subscription plan details (plan name, expiry date, file limits) to enforce plan quotas and display your usage accurately. Crash and performance data: We collect anonymous crash reports and performance traces to fix bugs and improve app stability. This data does not identify you personally. Analytics: We collect anonymous, aggregated usage events (e.g., "extraction started"). No personally identifiable information is attached to these events. 3. Payslip Files and Extracted Data Uploaded PDF files are sent to our secure AI processing service for text extraction. We do not store your PDF files on our servers. Extracted payslip data exists only on your device during your session and is exported by you to Excel. We do not retain, sell, or transmit your payslip contents. 4. How We Use Your Information To authenticate you and maintain your account To enforce your subscription plan's file limits To display your usage and plan status within the app To send a password reset email if requested To diagnose crashes and improve app performance 5. Data Sharing We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with trusted third-party infrastructure providers necessary to operate the app. All providers are bound by strict data protection agreements. 6. Data Retention Your account data (email, plan details, usage count) is retained as long as your account exists. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us at the email below. 7. Security We use industry-standard cloud security infrastructure. Access controls ensure that only you can read or modify your own account data. 8. Children's Privacy This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. 9. Your Rights You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us. We will respond within 30 days. 10.
